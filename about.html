About Finomate AI

We're reinventing how teams manage cloud costs

FinomateAI was born from a simple frustration: cloud cost tools show you the problem but don't help you fix it. We're changing that.

Our Story

Built by FinOps practitioners, for FinOps practitioners

We've spent years on the other side of the table — wrestling with fragmented dashboards, stale spreadsheets, and cost reports that arrived weeks too late to drive action.

FinomateAI is the platform we wished existed: one that unifies cost data across every cloud provider, surfaces AI-powered recommendations in real time, and actually helps teams implement savings — not just report on them.
